# hey, i'm David

> Kindness is the greatest gift you can give anyone.

Hello! My name is David, I'm a 15 year old writer and musician who is currently a high school student. I've had a very odd life, but I guess now's the time to reflect and look back at how my life has been.

Let's start with some background information!
- I go by many aliases, some being "cups", "dabido", and "gingerbag" (but no one calls me Gingerbag).
- I enjoy a variety of music, mostly indie and art pop, rock, ballads, and bedroom pop. I also listen to mainstream pop as well, but not often.
- Writing is mostly a hobby of mine, and I write in my spare time. As of recently, my writing has mostly been philosophy and thought-provoking pieces. I used to write a lot of fiction back then though.
- Music is also another hobby of mine that I focus on quite deeply. If you ask any of my real life friends and classmates, they'll say that I always bring my guitar to school. 90% of the time, I do. I don't know why, LOL.
- Finally, I enjoy quite a variety of food and drinks such as iced matcha (yes, I am that one guy who drinks matcha all the time), ramen (mainly "tantanmen"), and fried chicken.

Now, all of this information didn't form overnight. It took time, experience, and patience (even though I lack a lot of it) to allow for all of this to happen. Let's start at the beginning.

## Upbringing
I was born in the Philippines in the big '09. My life back then wasn't the most pleasant, but it wasn't *unbearable* either. I met some friends which I miss in a way, almost like a small memory missing from my mind. I had some family issues which didn't really turn out to be the most pleasant experience. I  grew up in a private school and I guess that was fun.

I was also raised Catholic, and I was baptised and confirmed as well. Even the private school I went to was Catholic, its basis was formed from a saint.

## Moving to Australia
In 2018, I moved to Australia with the rest of my family (which is just me, my dad, and my mum). My mum received a job that would change my life forever, through my personality, experiences, and perspectives on the world. I really wouldn't want it in any other way.
